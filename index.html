In our wild world we have a lot of good events to attend. Hundreds of posters are on every wall and sometimes its just impossible to copy all information from them to your calendar by hand. What you usually do? You just photo poster and hope that somehow later today you will remember to open the image and tranfer info from poster to your calendar. We propose another solution: Skalat. App which will help you to keep all posters about all events, and transfer all infor from them to your calendar. Of course we can offer much more than just this: keywords search, voting system, auto tagging, cloud sync and storage and more!

We are planning to make the app available for everybody with premium access to some of the functions. Your donation can help us to make this app real. Each dollar will give you access to some premium functions and cloud, limited to 100 posters. Each 5 dollars will give you access to all premium fuctions and limitless cloud.

We are the team which consists of two programmers, who are interested to change the way we interact with printed advertisement. We are alumnes of MIT who have experience in app development as well as in Natural Language Processing and Computer Vision.
